02 — Our story

From a backyard hobby to a 2.5-hectare farm.

George Allen Callora grew up around plants — his family kept ornamental plants and orchids. In 2016, growing chili peppers as a hobby turned into Nesta's Chili Company, named in memory of his aunt.

What started with ₱1,000 and a 20-square-meter greenhouse is now a 2.5-hectare farm, harvesting 250–300 kilos of superhot chili every two to three months — supplying raw chili to hot sauce makers, restaurants and franchises across Davao City.

During a crisis, we can slow down, but never back down.

— George Allen Callora, Founder
